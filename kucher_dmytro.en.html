Teaching methodology

Communicative technique in combination with author's methods to achieve maximum effect. The technique allows both train automatic usage of grammar and vocabulary, and to understand the mentality of native speakers. Classes are held taking into account the individual psycho-physical characteristics and capabilities of students, taking into account age and health. The groups are designed for students of different levels and ages. The selection of courses, the number of classes and their duration depend on the goals, abilities and financial situation of the students.

Teaching and levels

The training course consists of 6 levels in accordance with European requirements:

  • A1 Beginner The student understands and uses basic everyday words and expressions (greetings, introductions, family, professions, etc.). Understands people who speak clearly and slowly on certain topics.

  • A2 Elementary (elementary) The student can provide information about himself, tell about hobbies, navigate the territory, ask for necessary information.

  • B1 Intermediate The student can communicate on topics such as studies, free time, hobbies, can make contact and talk on most topics related to traveling abroad and related situations, can talk freely about himself without a noticeable lack of funds for this, he can describe his experiences, dreams, plans, briefly give arguments for and against.

  • B2 Upper intermediate The student can understand the main idea of a complex text on abstract and concrete topics, including discussions on technical topics. Free communication with a native speaker without significant difficulties. Can write a well-thought-out and logically structured text on a topic.

  • C1 Advanced The student can understand the implicit points of the content of the text. Uses the necessary linking words to connect the components of a sentence.

  • C2 Mastery or proficiency (Very advanced) The student can understand almost everything he reads or hears, can express himself spontaneously and quickly, distinguishing shades of meaning of phrases and expressions.

    • Time of learning depends on different levels. All this is discussed with the teacher individually. Early transfer to a higher level is possible, as well as being on same levels in cases if, for example, a student fails a test or an exam.
